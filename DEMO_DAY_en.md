# NLP Project Demo Day

## Overview

- **Date**: March 31, 2026 (Tuesday)
- **Time**: 13:30 - 17:45
- **Location**: 620-B
- **Format**: Group project presentations + Investment-based scoring

## Process

### Phase 1: Project Presentations

- Each group takes turns presenting their NLP project
- All presentations must be completed before the scoring phase begins

### Phase 2: Investment Scoring

1. Each group receives an equal amount of **virtual investment funds** (e.g., 1,000,000)
2. Each group must allocate all of their funds to **other groups** (you cannot invest in your own group)
3. Allocation is entirely free — you may concentrate or diversify your investments
4. Investments are submitted **anonymously**
5. All groups submit their allocation simultaneously

### Phase 3: Results & Grading

- Total investment received by each group is tallied
- A leaderboard is generated based on investment rankings
- **Final grades are determined by the instructor based on investment results**

## Rules

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| No self-investment | Each group may only allocate funds to other groups |
| Full allocation required | The entire fund must be distributed — no holding back |
| Anonymous submission | Investment plans are submitted anonymously to avoid social pressure |
| Present first, invest after | All presentations must finish before the investment round |
| Instructor's discretion | The instructor reserves the right to adjust final grades based on overall assessment |
